Onitama

Onitama Rules:

Onitama was designed by Shimpei Sato and published by Arane Wonders in 2014. While related to chess, Onitama gives the players a completely different experience. Any pawn can be moved in any way, as long as it follows the rules of one of the movement cards available to you!

There are two ways to win:

Way of Stone: Capture the opponent's Sage. Way of Water: Move your Sage to your opponent's temple.

Each turn, choose one of the movement cards available to you, then select a pawn. You can choose a new pawn if you like, or switch moevement cards and consider your different pawns again, before choosing a highlighted square to move.

Capture an enemy by moving one of your pawns, or your Sage, onto its square.
